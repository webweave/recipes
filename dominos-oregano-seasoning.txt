
Ingredients:
2 tbsp – Oregano flakes(or Italian seasoning)
1 tsp – Garlic powder
1/2 tsp – Pepper powder
2 – 3 drops of water
Salt to taste

Method:
Mix all the ingredients well. Once mixed add 2-3 drops of water and again mix well. Your Domino’s Oregano is ready. Simple isn’t it.
